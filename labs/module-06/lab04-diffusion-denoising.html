04 · Diffusion Denoising

A diffusion model starts from pure random noise and removes a little noise at each step, guided by your prompt, until a coherent image emerges. Press Generate to watch noise resolve into the chosen scene. More steps = finer detail; guidance controls how strongly it commits to the target.

Ready — press Generate.
Real diffusion predicts the noise to subtract at each step inside a learned network (U-Net/DiT), conditioned on the text prompt via cross-attention. This is a faithful visual analogy.
