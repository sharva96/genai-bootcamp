# TimeFlow X1 — Specifications

## Battery and charging
The TimeFlow X1 has a battery life of up to 7 days on a single charge with
typical use, or about 18 hours with continuous GPS tracking. It charges via a
magnetic pogo-pin dock and reaches 80% in 45 minutes. There is no wireless Qi
charging.

## Water resistance
The TimeFlow X1 is water resistant to 50 meters (5 ATM) and is safe for
swimming and showering. It is not rated for scuba diving or high-velocity water
sports such as wakeboarding.

## Sensors and connectivity
The X1 includes built-in GPS, a heart-rate sensor, an SpO2 (blood-oxygen)
monitor, an accelerometer, and a skin-temperature sensor. It connects to a
phone over Bluetooth 5.2 and supports Wi-Fi for software updates.

## Display
The X1 has a 1.4-inch AMOLED display with an always-on mode. Brightness reaches
1000 nits, so it remains readable in direct sunlight.
