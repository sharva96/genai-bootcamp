# Warranty and Returns

## Limited warranty
The TimeFlow X1 ships with a 1-year limited warranty against manufacturing
defects. The warranty covers hardware faults but excludes accidental damage,
water damage beyond the rated 50 meters, and normal wear of the band.

## Extended warranty
TimeFlow Care is an optional plan that extends coverage to 3 years and adds two
accidental-damage repairs. It must be purchased within 30 days of the watch
purchase.

## Returns
Watches bought directly from TimeFlow can be returned within 30 days for a full
refund, provided they are in original condition with all accessories. Refunds
are processed to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days.

## Repairs
For a warranty repair, start a claim in the app under Settings > Support. You
will receive a prepaid shipping label. Out-of-warranty repairs are quoted before
any work begins.
