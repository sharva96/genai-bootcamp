Standard Models vs Reasoning Models New in 2025

A major shift in 2024-2025: models that explicitly "think" before responding. Pick the right type for your task.

⚡ Standard Generative Models

Respond immediately, optimized for speed and throughput
  • Fast (sub-second to a few seconds)
  • Lower cost per query
  • Best for chat, writing, summarization, extraction
  • Most production traffic goes here
  • Generally sufficient for 80% of real-world tasks
GPT-5Claude Sonnet 4.6Gemini 2.5 FlashLlama 4 ScoutGPT-5 mini

🧠 Reasoning Models

Deliberate before responding — "think then answer"
  • Slower (seconds to minutes of "thinking" before output)
  • 10-100× more expensive per query
  • Best for math, complex coding, research, planning
  • Dramatically higher accuracy on hard tasks
  • Use only when speed/cost is justified by accuracy need

How to Choose a Model in 2026

Six common scenarios and the recommended approach.

🚀 General chat / writing / summarization

High-volume general-purpose AI assistant work.

Pick: Claude Sonnet 4.6 or GPT-5 via API. Both deliver excellent quality at ~$3/M input. Use the cheaper GPT-5 mini or Gemini 2.5 Flash if cost matters more than top quality.

🧠 Hard reasoning / math / research

Tasks where one wrong answer is worse than 10× the latency.

Pick: OpenAI o3, Claude Opus 4.7 with extended thinking, or DeepSeek-R1 (open). Expect minutes per response and 10-50× the cost — but accuracy gains can be dramatic.

📚 Long-context analysis

Processing entire codebases, books, video, or thousands of pages.

Pick: Gemini 2.5 Pro (2M token context — industry-leading) or Claude Sonnet 4.6 (500K tokens with strong long-context coherence).

💻 Coding / agentic dev work

Building features, fixing bugs, multi-file refactors.

Pick: Claude Opus 4.7 or Sonnet 4.6 — currently dominant on SWE-bench Verified. Used inside Claude Code, Cursor, and most AI coding tools.

🔒 Privacy / on-premises / sovereignty

Data cannot leave your infrastructure (healthcare, defense, regulated industries).

Pick: Llama 4 Maverick / Scout (open weights, commercial use allowed) or Mistral Large 2 (European, GDPR-aligned). Self-host on your own VPC.

💸 Cost-sensitive at scale

Millions of calls per day, cost is the primary constraint.

Pick: GPT-5 mini ($0.25/M), Gemini 2.5 Flash ($0.30/M), or DeepSeek-V3 ($0.27/M). All 30-50× cheaper than frontier models with surprisingly strong quality.
