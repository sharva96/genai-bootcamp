04 · Content Moderation Scores

A moderation model (like OpenAI's omni-moderation-latest) returns a score per harm category, not just a yes/no. The right move is to set your own threshold per your risk tolerance — a kids' app blocks far lower than a creative-writing tool. Pick a sample, then drag the threshold and watch what flips from clean to flagged.
