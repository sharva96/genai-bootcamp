05 · AI Governance — EU AI Act Risk Tiers

The EU AI Act classifies systems by risk, and obligations scale with the tier. Pick a system on the left to see where it lands and what you'd owe — or click a tier in the pyramid to read its rules. Most chatbots are limited risk: the binding duty is disclosure. See Guide 07 §10.
