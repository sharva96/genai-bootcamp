# TimeFlow Companion App

## Compatibility
The TimeFlow app syncs with both iOS (15 and later) and Android (10 and later).
A single account can pair up to three TimeFlow devices at once.

## Pairing a new watch
Open the app, tap "Add Device", and hold the watch within 10 cm of the phone.
Confirm the six-digit code shown on both screens. Pairing fails if Bluetooth is
off or if the watch battery is below 5%.

## Syncing health data
Health metrics sync automatically every 15 minutes when the watch is in
Bluetooth range. You can force a sync by pulling down on the app's home screen.
Data is stored encrypted and can be exported as CSV from Settings > Privacy.

## Troubleshooting sync
If the watch stops syncing, toggle Bluetooth off and on, then restart the app.
If that fails, unpair and re-pair the device. Firmware updates require a Wi-Fi
connection and at least 30% battery.
