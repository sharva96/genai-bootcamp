TimeFlow X1 — Quick Tips (plain text)

Getting started
Charge the watch fully before first use — about 90 minutes on the included
magnetic puck. Press and hold the side button for three seconds to power on,
then follow the on-watch pairing prompt.

Daily use
Swipe down for quick settings, swipe up for notifications. A single press of the
crown returns you to the watch face from anywhere. Double-press the crown to jump
to your most recent app.

Battery saving
Turn on Always-On Display only when you need it — leaving it off can add up to a
full day of battery life. Lowering screen brightness and disabling continuous
heart-rate tracking also stretch the battery between charges.

Troubleshooting
If the watch becomes unresponsive, hold both the crown and the side button for
ten seconds to force a restart. This does not erase your data or settings.
